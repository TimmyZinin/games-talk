05 · GDD
09 / 12
EN

Game Design Doc.
Six sections, max.

A GDD is not a 200-page bible. In 2026 it's a living markdown file you write with Claude. Done well, it makes every later prompt 10× sharper.

TR

Oyun Tasarım Belgesi.
En fazla altı bölüm.

GDD 200 sayfalık kutsal kitap değildir. 2026'da Claude ile birlikte yazdığınız canlı bir markdown dosyasıdır. İyi yazılırsa sonraki tüm prompt'lar 10 kat keskin olur.

  • 1 · Pitch — name, genre, platform, core loop in one sentence
  • 2 · Mechanics — actions, controls, progression, fail/win
  • 3 · Art — style, references, mood board, palette
  • 4 · Narrative — world, characters, tone, pacing
  • 5 · UI & Audio — wireframes, music brief, SFX list
  • 6 · Scope — MVP, monetization, ship date, what's out

Pro tip · Ask Claude: "Push back on this GDD. Where will I get stuck in week two?"

Live document·Edit it as the game changes

WORLD LABS · Nov 2025 Marble Photo or prompt → 3D world. Gaussian splats & mesh export. Editable, downloadable, embeddable.
GOOGLE DEEPMIND · 2026 Genie 3 Real-time playable worlds. 720p · 24fps · ~60s to generate. Walk through a sentence.
DECART Mirage Real-time video-to-video world transformation. Modify a world while you play it.

For us, today: Marble already exports walkable scenes you can drop into Three.js or Unity. The future is partly here — the cheap part.
